YAO is an izakaya and vinyl listening bar in Todos Santos, Baja California Sur. Contemporary Asian kitchen, curated records, and a program of resident DJs and guest artists pushing the local scene forward.

Not a restaurant with music. Not a bar with food. Something without a clean category — and that's exactly the point.

The Experience

Curated.
Intimate.
Unrepeatable.

Every omakase at YAO is a single sitting — a conversation between the kitchen, the vinyl, and the people at the bar. No two nights are the same. Japanese technique, Baja ingredients, and a soundtrack chosen by hand.
